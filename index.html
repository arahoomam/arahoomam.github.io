Research

UIUC PURE: Mining Potential Relations from Biomedical Data

Overall research problem is to build an open entity relation centric extraction and ranking system in the form of a web-scalable search engine platform within the medical domain (focusing on chemicals and diseases) by mining millions of paper abstracts for relations

My work focused on creating a relation evaluation model by ranking a relation phrase with an entity pair, essentially being able to accurately predict what the user would most likely want to see
